## Overview

The **Social Change system** is the core mechanical framework of the Housing Crisis campaign. Your goal isn't just to fight—it's to transform Paradise Heights from a war zone into a thriving community.

## The Eight Statistics

Paradise Heights is tracked using eight neighborhood statistics, each rated from 0-10:

### 1. Safety
How secure residents feel. Are streets safe to walk at night? Can children play outside?

**Low Safety:** Gang violence, muggings, random shootings  
**High Safety:** Residents patrol neighborhoods, community watches established

### 2. Employment
Job availability and economic opportunity for residents.

**Low Employment:** Widespread unemployment, desperation, poverty  
**High Employment:** Local businesses hiring, job training programs, economic activity

### 3. Health
Access to medical care, clean water, and sanitation.

**Low Health:** Contaminated water, disease, no medical facilities  
**High Health:** Clinic established, clean water, preventive care available

### 4. Infrastructure
Condition of buildings, utilities, and public spaces.

**Low Infrastructure:** Broken elevators, no power, crumbling buildings  
**High Infrastructure:** Reliable utilities, maintained buildings, functional amenities

### 5. Education
Access to learning, skills training, and information.

**Low Education:** No schools, illiteracy, lack of information  
**High Education:** Schools operating, libraries, vocational training

### 6. Morale
Community spirit, hope for the future, and social cohesion.

**Low Morale:** Despair, apathy, every-person-for-themselves  
**High Morale:** Community pride, mutual support, hope for improvement

### 7. Crime
Level of criminal activity beyond gang violence (theft, smuggling, exploitation).

**High Crime:** Rampant theft, black markets, exploitation of vulnerable  
**Low Crime:** Honest community, mutual respect, trust

### 8. Corruption
Degree of bribery, extortion, and corrupt officials.

**High Corruption:** Rent-A-Cops extorting, officials taking bribes, price gouging  
**Low Corruption:** Fair treatment, accountability, community oversight

## Starting Conditions

**Paradise Heights**
- Safety: 2
- Employment: 1
- Health: 1
- Infrastructure: 2
- Education: 1
- Morale: 2
- Crime: 9
- Corruption: 8

## Improving Statistics

### Actions That Improve Stats

**Safety:**
- Clear out gang strongholds
- Establish neighborhood watch
- Drive out corrupt Rent-A-Cops
- Create safe gathering spaces

**Employment:**
- Establish local businesses
- Create job training programs
- Secure trade agreements with nearby zones
- Clear gang control over work opportunities

**Health:**
- Secure clean water supply
- Establish medical clinic
- Liberate medical supplies
- Train community medics

**Infrastructure:**
- Repair utilities (power/water/elevators)
- Fix building damage
- Create community spaces
- Establish waste management

**Education:**
- Create community school
- Establish library/information center
- Skills training workshops
- Youth programs

**Morale:**
- Win major victories
- Host community events
- Achieve visible improvements
- Defend against corporate attacks successfully

**Crime:**
- Eliminate black market exploitation
- Provide legitimate alternatives to crime
- Community justice system
- Remove criminal leadership

**Corruption:**
- Drive out corrupt officials
- Establish oversight mechanisms
- Fair resource distribution system
- Community accountability

### Downtime Actions

Between missions, players can take **Downtime Actions** to improve stats. Each character gets one action per session:

**Community Building:**
- Organize neighborhood meetings
- Train residents in useful skills
- Establish social programs

**Resource Acquisition:**
- Scavenge for supplies
- "Liberate" corporate resources
- Negotiate with traders

**Relationship Building:**
- Gain trust of residents
- Build alliances with other zones
- Recruit volunteers

**Personal Development:**
- Train skills
- Research problems
- Rest and recover

## Stat Degradation

**Warning:** Stats can decrease if neglected or undermined:

- **Safety** decreases if gangs retaliate or new threats emerge
- **Health** decreases if water/medicine supplies run out
- **Morale** decreases after major defeats or betrayals
- **Crime** increases if desperation grows (low Employment/Health)
- **Corruption** increases if resources become scarce

## Milestone Rewards

Achieving certain thresholds unlocks benefits:

**QoL 20+:**
- "Stabilized" - residents begin to trust you
- Unlock: Community volunteers assist missions
- Morale boost: +1 to all social rolls in Paradise Heights

**QoL 30+:**
- "Improving" - Paradise Heights attracts attention (good and bad)
- Unlock: Local businesses provide resources/intelligence
- Warning: Corporate interest increases

**QoL 40+:**
- "Thriving" - Paradise Heights is a model community
- Unlock: Residents actively defend their home
- Warning: Corporations want their land back
